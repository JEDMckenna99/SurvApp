## Core Scheduling & Dispatching

### Drag & Drop Scheduling
- User-friendly calendar interface for visual job management
- Easy assignment of jobs to technicians
- Set arrival windows with customizable time slots
- Organize and view calendar by day, week, or month

### Real-Time Dispatching
- Instant notifications to technicians for new jobs
- Automatic updates when job details change
- Push notifications to mobile devices
- Job status tracking in real-time

### Recurring Jobs Management
- Set up recurring appointments for regular customers
- Automated scheduling for maintenance plans
- Custom recurrence patterns (daily, weekly, monthly, custom)
- Automatic customer notifications for recurring services

### Offline Scheduling Access
- Technicians can access job data without internet connection
- Sync capabilities when connection is restored
- Minimizes service interruptions in areas with poor reception
- Cached job information available on mobile devices

## Summary

HouseCall Pro provides a comprehensive field service management solution with features spanning:

- **Operational Efficiency**: Scheduling, dispatching, and job management
- **Financial Management**: Invoicing, payments, and reporting
- **Customer Experience**: CRM, online booking, and communication tools
- **Marketing**: Email campaigns, online presence, and review management
- **Team Management**: Time tracking, GPS monitoring, and employee tools
- **Business Intelligence**: Analytics, reporting, and insights
- **Integration**: QuickBooks, Zapier, and third-party connections
- **Mobile Access**: Full-featured iOS and Android applications

The platform is designed to help home service businesses operate more efficiently, enhance customer satisfaction, and drive revenue growth through automation, streamlined processes, and comprehensive business management tools.
